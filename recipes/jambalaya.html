Jambalaya

Description

Jambalaya is an American Creole and Cajun rice dish of French (especially Proven√ßal cuisine), African, and Spanish influence, consisting mainly of meat and vegetables mixed with rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • 1/2 lb chicken
  • 1/2 lb sausage
  • 1/2 lb peeled shrimp
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1 green pepper

Steps

  1. Prep the onion, celery and green pepper by slicing them.
  2. In a large pot, grill the chicken with the onion, celery, and bell pepper.
  3. Toss in sausages and shrimp once chicken is cooked halfway.
  4. Pour the can of crushed tomatoes into the pot along with the rice and 4.5 cups of water.
  5. Cover pot with lid and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce to a simmer and let sit for 25 min.
  7. Plate and serve for 4.
